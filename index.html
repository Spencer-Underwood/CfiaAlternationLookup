Appendix B (Employment Transition Policy) - Article 6.3: Alternation

Per Article 6.3.7, an alternation can occur when positions are considered equivalent. This is defined as a situation where the maximum rate of pay for the higher-paid position is no more than six percent (6%) higher than the maximum rate of pay for the lower-paid position.
